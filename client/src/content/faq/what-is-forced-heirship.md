---
question: "What is forced heirship in Louisiana?"
category: "Louisiana Law"
order: 3
---
Louisiana is the only state in the United States with forced heirship laws. These laws require a parent to leave a portion of their estate (called the 'forced portion') to children who are under 24 years old or who are permanently incapacitated — regardless of what the will says.
