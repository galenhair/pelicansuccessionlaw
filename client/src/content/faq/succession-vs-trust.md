---
question: "What is the difference between a succession and a trust?"
category: "Louisiana Law"
order: 11
---
A succession is a court-supervised process that transfers assets after death when no trust exists. A trust is a planning tool that allows assets to pass to beneficiaries without going through court. If the deceased had a properly funded revocable living trust, a succession may not be necessary for those assets. We can help you determine whether a succession is required.
