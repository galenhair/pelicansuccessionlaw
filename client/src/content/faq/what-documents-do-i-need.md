---
question: "What documents do I need to start a succession?"
category: "Documents"
order: 6
---
The core documents are: (1) a certified copy of the death certificate, (2) the original Last Will and Testament if one exists, (3) the deed or tax assessment for any real estate, and (4) basic information about the heirs (names, addresses, and relationship to the deceased). We will send you a complete checklist after your free consultation.
